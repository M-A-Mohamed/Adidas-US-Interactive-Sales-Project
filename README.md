## üîç Dashboard Components

### 1. Total Sales by Product Category (Donut Chart)
Provides a percentage breakdown of sales across major product lines:
*   **Men's Street Footwear:** 23%
*   **Women's Apparel:** 20%
*   **Men's Athletic Footwear:** 17%
*   **Men's Apparel:** 14%
*   **Women's Street Footwear:** 14%
*   **Women's Athletic Footwear:** 12%

### 2. Operating Profit Analysis (Bar Chart)
Visualizes the total operating profit generated by each category, highlighting **Men's Street Footwear** as the most profitable segment ($82.8M).

### 3. Regional Performance (Line Chart)
Tracks the "Count of unit sold per region" to identify geographic demand:
*   **West:** 2,448 units
*   **Northeast:** 2,376 units
*   **Midwest:** 1,872 units
*   **South:** 1,728 units
*   **Southeast:** 1,224 units

### 4. Quarterly Sales Trends (Time Series)
Compares Total Sales per Quarter for the years **2020** and **2021**. The data shows a significant upward trend in 2021 compared to the previous year, peaking in the third quarter.

### 5. Sales Method per Retailer (Stacked Bar Chart)
A detailed breakdown of how different retailers (**Amazon, Foot Locker, Kohl's, Sports Direct, Walmart, and West Gear**) generate sales via:
*   **In-store**
*   **Online**
*   **Outlet**
